About Us

DuoFine is a Turkey-based technology startup, founded by two young co-founders with a passion for pushing boundaries. Our core mission is to solve complex problems with clean, elegant software, creating meaningful connections and experiences through technology.

Driven by Vision.

We are not just building apps; we are crafting experiences. Our focus is on intuitive design and robust engineering to create products that are both powerful and delightful to use.

Our Flagship Project

Common Ground

Common Ground is a platform engineered to bring people together under the most optimal, fun, and efficient conditions based on their location. Empowering users with features like instant messaging, live location tracking, and a seamless meetup evaluation system, our application maximizes the potential for meaningful connections.
