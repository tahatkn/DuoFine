Privacy Policy

Effective date: 8 August 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Duofine ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application Common Ground and visit duofine.com.

We collect only what we need to run our services, we don’t sell your data, and you’re in control of your information.

Who we are

Duofine operates the Common Ground mobile application and duofine.com. For the purposes of the EU/UK GDPR, Duofine is the controller of your personal data when you use our products and website.

Scope

This policy applies to:

  • Our mobile app Common Ground (iOS/Android).
  • Our website duofine.com, including subpages.
  • Customer support and communications (e.g., email, forms).

Data we collect

Information you provide

  • Account details (name, email, password or SSO identifiers).
  • Profile data (e.g., avatar, preferences).
  • Content you submit (messages, forms, feedback).
  • Billing data for purchases (handled by Apple/Google; we receive confirmation metadata only).

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and diagnostic data (app actions, crash logs, performance metrics).
  • Device data and location.
  • Cookies and similar technologies on our website (see “Cookies” below).

Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies to operate the site, and—where applicable—analytics cookies to improve our services. Where required by law, we will request your consent before using non-essential cookies.

How we use data

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Common Ground and duofine.com.
  • Authenticate users, prevent fraud, and secure our services.
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions (via Apple/Google).
  • Provide customer support and communicate important updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Legal bases (GDPR/UK GDPR)

  • Contract: to deliver the app and website features you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to keep our services secure, prevent abuse, and improve performance.
  • Consent: for optional analytics, marketing, or where required (you can withdraw anytime).
  • Legal obligation: to meet regulatory and tax requirements.

Sharing & processors

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with trusted service providers acting on our behalf ("processors"), such as:

  • Cloud hosting & infrastructure (e.g., application hosting, databases, content storage).
  • Analytics & crash reporting (to improve stability and performance).
  • Email & notifications (to deliver account and service messages).
  • App store platforms (Apple App Store / Google Play for payments & distribution).

Where we use processors, we have agreements in place requiring them to protect your data and use it only under our instructions.

Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, and as required by law. When no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize the data.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures to safeguard personal data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we strive to protect your data and continuously improve our practices.

International transfers

Where data is transferred outside your country (for example, to the EEA, UK, or the United States), we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or other lawful mechanisms.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object to certain processing. You may also withdraw consent where we rely on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@duofine.com. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version on this page and update the effective date above. If changes are material, we will provide additional notice as required.

Contact us

For any questions or requests about this policy, contact:
